Private / temporary phone numbers

Keep your own phone number private, and give out your temporary phone number to manage your event. This way you won't get bombarded with phone calls once your event is over.

Reasons to Get a Private Number
  • Hosting an Event, such as charity auction, wedding
  • Conference calls
  • Selling your home, automobile etc. Put your temporary number in the paper and keep your personal phone number private
  • Managing a Group, such as book club, PTA, church group or boy scouts
  • Give out while dating or meeting new friends
